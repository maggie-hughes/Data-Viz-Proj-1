The Guardian's Response to American Terror Attacks

In this study, I look at the Guardian's report on four mass shootings in the US: Las Vegas, Orlando, San Bernardino, and Charleston. Then, I measure how the Guardian distribute's its coverage on these shootings, the language they use to describe each shooting, and how the language changes based on the demographic of the shooter and those who died.
