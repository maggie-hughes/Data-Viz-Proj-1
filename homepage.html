The Guardian's Response to American Terror Attacks

American media is infamous for filtering its language when reporting on terror attacks and mass shootings depending on the demographic of the shooter and those who died. Here, I measure the number of articles that use specific language that suggests the articles link the attacks with specific topic: Demographics informaiton, Mental Health, Politics, Gun Rights, Terror, and Weapons.
